Transform your ideas into structured business intelligence using SWOT framework.

SWOT stands for Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. It is a strategic planning technique used to evaluate business ideas, startups, and projects. It helps you understand internal factors (Strengths & Weaknesses) and external factors (Opportunities & Threats).
