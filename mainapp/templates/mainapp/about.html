What is SWOT Analysis?

SWOT stands for Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. It is a strategic planning method used to evaluate business ideas, startups, and projects.

Strengths

Internal advantages of your idea such as innovation, skills, resources, or technology that give you an edge.

Weaknesses

Internal limitations such as lack of experience, funding, or technical gaps that may slow down success.

Opportunities

External factors like market demand, trends, or technology growth that can help your idea expand.

Threats

External risks such as competition, market changes, or regulatory challenges that may impact success.
