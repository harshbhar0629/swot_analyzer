⚙️ How It Works

A simple workflow that converts your idea into a structured AI SWOT analysis

🔄 Step-by-Step Process
1. Create Account
Register and log in to access the SWOT Analyzer system.
2. Choose Category
Select a relevant category to help the AI understand your domain better.
3. Enter Your Idea
Provide your startup or business idea in detail.
4. AI Analysis
The system sends your idea to an AI model which generates: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats.
5. View Results
Get structured insights along with explanations and suggestions.
6. Feedback
Share feedback to improve the system and analysis quality.
🎯 Purpose

The goal of this system is to help students, entrepreneurs, and startups quickly evaluate ideas before investing time, money, or resources.
